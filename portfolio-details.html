    Gearbox Academy

    Is a training institution that focuses on imparting relevant hands on skills through Human Centered Design (HCD) and Industry 4.0 technologies, to promote digital literacy in engineering fields.

    We helped train students to identify challenges in our society, then come up with effective and practical solutions. The students therefore apply HCD in ideation to make solutions meet the users need, then implement them by applying industry 4 technologies such as Embedded systems or Digital design and Fabrication.

    Through coming up with several projects together with the students, getting to have hands-on experience with building the projects from scratch and iterating them to final end products has increased our overall knowledge and skills.
